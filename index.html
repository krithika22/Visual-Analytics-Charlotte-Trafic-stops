Intro

We are a team of three people - Akash, Amparo, Krithika working with the Charlotte Traffic stops database. There has been a huge number of crimes and victimizations happening in several traffic stops due to improper driving. In order to find an optimal way to deploy officers based on the level of incidents in an area, we have created a couple of visualizations. While our main purpose is said, we also found several interesting insights from the data. Some correlations between the parameters of the database were drawn and the trend was analyzed. We were able to learn some relationships we did not see in the data before, which could be used to solve more problems than the one addressed.
